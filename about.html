About Me

My name is Teresa Godat, and I am a wife and mother. I work full-time as a software tester and am studying to become a web developer. The hardest job I've experienced is being a parent; yet at the same time it is the most rewarding.

My son is so smart! He can be so loving and is super silly. This past year has been all sorts of challenges and working on overcoming those challenges.

Things were going pretty good for about a month at the new school. My son quickly started acting out, had a hard time listening and then started getting very angry.
